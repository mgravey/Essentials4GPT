Double Click Settings

History Editing

Allow editing of history and archive or delete multiple conversations at once.

Keyboard Behavior

Switch Enter and Shift+Enter behaviors. Pressing Enter will move to the next line and Shift+Enter will send the message.

Clipboard Behavior

Modify pasting behavior to avoid pasting text and images together from Word. Only text will be pasted if the clipboard contains text and images.

Enable Automatic Expiration

Sets an automatic expiration date based on last activity. Conversations will be automatically deleted after the set number of days unless disabled or adjusted per conversation.
